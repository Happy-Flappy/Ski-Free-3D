Candivores is a Pseudo 3d game about hunting down the heavily sought after candy creatures of Candivore Land. 

Creatures featured:


Marshmello - Safe non-hunted
Striped hard candy - Safe non-hunted 
Striped Peppermint balls - dangerous - duplicates itself as it chases you - bounces around a lot
Candy Cane - safe grazing creature - tilts its head downwards to scrape the snow with its hook when looking for food. Uses its hands to pick up what it found.
Hot chocolate monster - dangerous carnivore
Gingerbread Man - dangerous carnivore
Snowman - Extremely dangerous carnivore



Decorations:

Stuff from explo png such as (lollypops,architechtural items)
Foliage
Candy cane forest - home to the candy cane grazer


Actions:

Map with hunted creature location.
Trophy room
Healthbar above creatures
Blowtorch gun - limited damage
Glue gun - slows down creatures

Temporary powerups like speedy eggbert - lollypop gives speed(with sparkles trail) and jump
